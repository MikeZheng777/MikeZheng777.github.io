Research

I'm interested in scalable planning and decision-making for multi-agent systems, combining reinforcement learning with classical search and combinatorial optimization. My work spans multi-agent path finding (MAPF), autonomous driving coordination, warehouse robotics, and LLM-powered optimization solvers.
